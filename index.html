My Profile

I am a Web Developer and the Co-founder of Millennium Green Group Holdings, LLC (MGG Holdings) based in Atlanta, GA. where I serve as Vice President of Global Business Development establishimg Renewable Energy sites and MGG Holdings Technology Parks worldwide.

MGG Holdings has strategic partnership with governments, companies, universities and civil society organizations in the areas of sustainable development, agriculture, clean drinking water, bioprocessing technologies, clean cooking with sustainable biofuels, electrification solutions and “green” community development in Africa.

Prior to MGG Holdings, my career involves various research and development projects for bioanalytical application, including the development of pro-drug candidate for HIV-1 specific proteins inhibition under the Scientific Team of Professor Jean Claude Chermann, co-discover of the Human Immunodeficiency Virus-1 (HIV-1) and Near Infra-Red (NIR) fluorescence immunoassays for conventional applications; DNA probe techniques; pH, metal ion and hydrophobicity determinations. The most promising part of the material science research was the development of new spectroscopic method for forensic applications including a new latent blood detection method using new fluorescin chemistry and new analytical techniques for latent fingerprint detection and determination of other chemical residues, e.g., pepper spray residues, gunshot residues and explosive residues.
